Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add milk, oil, egg, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in boiling water until well combined.
  5. Pour batter into the cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
